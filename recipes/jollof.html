Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof, or jollof rice, is a rice dish from West Africa.
The dish is typically made with long-grain rice, tomatoes, onions, spices, vegetables and meat in a single pot
although its ingredients and preparation methods vary across different regions. Nigerian jollof is the greatest jollof rice there will ever be!

ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • chilli pepper, red pepper
  • Cooking oil
  • seasoning
  • spices, salt
  • curry powder and dried thyme
  • onions, carrots, green peas, sweet corn and whatever
  • assortment e.g beef, crayfish, meat, cow skin e.t.c

Steps

  1. parboil rice and set aside
  2. Blend your tomatoes and peppers for about 45secs
  3. heat oil in pot on medium heat
  4. add onions and fry until it turns golden yellow then add tomato paste
  5. add tomato mixture from step 1 and stir fry for about 10 minutes
  6. add seasoning and chicken stock and boil for 5 minutes
  7. Add the parboiled rice to the mixture and mix thoroughly
  8. add little water and required seasoning
  9. cover and let it cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  10. let it simmer and your jollof is ready to serve!
