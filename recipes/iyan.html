Iyan (Pounded Yam)

Description

Pounded yam or Iyán is a Nigerian food native to the Yoruba, Igbo, Ebira and Tiv ethnic groups.
It is a traditional food. It is prepared by pounding boiled yam with a mortar and pestle.
Pounded yam is similar to mashed potatoes but heavier. It is a smooth and tasty delicacy traditionally eaten with the hands.

Ingredients

  • yam or
  • plantain
  • mortar and pestle

Steps

  1. Peel the yam and cut it into small cubes
  2. rinse then boil for about 30 minutes until it is soft
  3. Clean the mortar and pestle by washing with water
  4. pound the yam until soft, smooth and dough-like.
  5. Eat íyán when it is hot and freshly served with your favorite soup
